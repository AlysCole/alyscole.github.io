About Me

It started with custom UIs built for text-based games, and themes for community forums. It wasn’t long before I dove into web development. Since then, I’ve worked on a number of projects spanning different industries, from online education to (currently) health care.

Today, I’m focused on building web applications with the user and good design in mind.

Outside of coding, I enjoy working on personal projects, cycling, a little bit of weightlifting, and reading fantasy and sci-fi on my spare time.
