It started with custom UIs built for text-based games, originally displayed white-on-black on a terminal. It wasn’t long before I dove into web development. Since then, I’ve worked on a number of projects spanning different industries, from online education to financial technology and, currently, health care.

Today, I’m focused on building web applications with the user and good design in mind.

Outside of coding, I enjoy different styles of cycling, a little bit of weightlifting, and reading fantasy and sci-fi on my spare time.
